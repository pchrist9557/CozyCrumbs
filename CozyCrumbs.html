Welcome

Cozy Crumbs Bakehouse - always striving to create a sweet cozy indulgence from the first bite to the crumbs. Specializing in Macarons, and Baking to Order in Oklahoma City.

We offer French Macarons upon order for the events, parties, holidays, gifts, or any occasion to just treat yourself!

What are Macarons?

Macs (aka macarons) are delightful french pastries made with very fine almond flour, egg whites, and confectioners sugar, using a special technique. With a smooth crisp exterior and a lovely chewey bite in the middle, these little desserts will make you feel cozy from the first bite to the last pieces of the crumbs.
